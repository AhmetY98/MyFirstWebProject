Famous Foods and Drinks of Istanbul

Scroll down to see amazing foods of Turkish culture

Turkish Tea is an important part of Turkish culture, and is the most commonly consumed hot drink, despite the country's long history of coffee consumption. Offering tea to guests is part of Turkish hospitality. Tea is most often consumed in households, shops

Kokorec is one of the famous street food in Turkey. It has a distinct look that not everyone can enjoy but if when tried it will definetly change your mind. It is made out of beef intestines which might sound ugly. However, the way it is cleaned and cooked will definetly change your mind. Also goes through a sensitive cleaning process to make sure that it is ready to eat.

Kebab is one of the famous food that is consumed in the world. It is known by almost every country in the world for its delicious taste. The distinct flavours of kebab is one of the most beatiful thing in the world.

Iskender is kind of a kebab that is called doner in turkish. It specailly cut extra thin to make sure that you get the best out of it. It also includes fried pita and yogurt. After the combination of these delicious ingredients you drizzle melted butter on top it to add extra flavours

Dolma is one of the most famous food in Turkish cuisine. It can be either consumed as an appetizer or as an entire entree. It is a food that comes all the way from the Ottoman Empire.
